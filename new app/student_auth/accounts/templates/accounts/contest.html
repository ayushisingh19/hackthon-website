About the Competition

The Sage Coding Competition is a premier competitive programming event designed to challenge students with a knack for problem-solving. This year's theme focuses on *dynamic programming and advanced algorithms*. Participants will face a series of complex problems that will test both their theoretical knowledge and practical coding skills.

  • 2nd Place: ₹15,000 Cash + Certificate of Excellence
  • 3rd Place: ₹10,000 Cash + Certificate of Excellence
